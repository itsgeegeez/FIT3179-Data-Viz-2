Malaysia General Election 2022

The 2022 Malaysian General Election map showcases a diverse political landscape across the country. Peninsular Malaysia displays a competitive mix of parties, while Borneo presents more regional dominance. This visualization highlights stark differences in political allegiances between the peninsula and Borneo states, reflecting Malaysia's complex cultural and demographic tapestry.

Top 10 states with highest average majority votes

This bar chart compares average majority votes across Malaysian states and federal territories. Selangor leads with the highest average, followed by Kuala Lumpur and Pulau Pinang. There's a significant range in voter majorities, from Selangor's ~32,000 votes to minimal margins in places like W.P. Labuan. The visualization reveals varied levels of electoral competitiveness across Malaysia's regions.
